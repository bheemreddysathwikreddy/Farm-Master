About Farming India

Farming India is a simple educational demo site that presents key information about crops, irrigation, modern farming technology and plant health relevant to Indian agriculture.

Our mission

To provide accessible, practical information about sustainable and modern farming methods that help farmers, students and enthusiasts learn core concepts and explore next steps for real-world adoption.

What we cover

  • Major crops grown across India and their growing regions.
  • Irrigation techniques and water-wise practices.
  • Robotic and precision-farming technologies.
  • Common crop diseases and basic management suggestions.
